# Business Context — North American Telecom Market

## Why this industry

Telecommunications is one of the most data-intensive regulated sectors in North America. Every major carrier reports to a federal regulator:

- **Canada:** CRTC (Canadian Radio-television and Telecommunications Commission)
- **United States:** FCC (Federal Communications Commission)

Strategy, analytics, and data platform teams at carriers and consultancies combine **regulatory market intelligence** with **internal subscriber operations** to track share, churn, ARPU, and regional performance.

| Quebecor Group | Videotron | Fizz (MVNO) | Regional challenger (Quebec) |

**Key CRTC finding (2025 market report):** The four largest service provider groups (Bell, TELUS, Rogers, Quebecor and flankers) accounted for **85.6%** of total telecommunications service revenues in 2023. Top 3 mobile operators held **86.9%** subscriber market share in 2023.

FCC Form 477 county data tracks fixed broadband connections by geography — the standard public source for US broadband deployment analysis.

## Business questions this pipeline answers

1. How is **mobile subscriber share** shifting by province (Canada) and by state (US)?
2. How do **synthetic operational churn rates** reconcile with CRTC-published Top 3 benchmarks (~1.16% monthly)?
3. What is the **cross-border view** for adjacent regions (BC/WA, ON/NY, MI)?
